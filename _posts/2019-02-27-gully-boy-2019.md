**_Gully Boy_** is one of those rarest movie in 2019 that had hit the chords of both critics and audience. _Zoya Akhtar_ chose to bring the rhythm of **Dharavi**, Mumbai into the mainstream and coined the movie title from an original song, [**_Mere Gully Mein_**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1bK5dzwhu-I) sung by [_Naezy_](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Naezy) and [_Divine_](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Divine_\(rapper\)). The story is an inspiration drawn from the lives of these two Indian Rappers, _Vivian Fernandez_ (_**Divine**_) and _Naved Shaikh_ (_**Naezy**_). For the promotion of the movie, they invited _**Nas**_ (an American Rapper), _**Divine**_, and _**Naezy**_ to release an audio [_**NY Se Mumbai**_](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cZSJRmd5gmg) with the movie lead, _Ranveer Singh_.

_Zoya Akhtar_ brings the story of _Murad_ (a boy from Dharavi streets) to the Indian audience the same way [_Curtis Hanson_](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Curtis_Hanson) did for the American audience. [_**Gully Boy**_](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt2395469/) is a movie that matches in bits and pieces with [_**8 Mile**_](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0298203/). A story of a local street boy who lives with his broken family and tries to make his way up. _**MC Bob**_ of [_**8 Mile**_](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0298203/) is nowhere near to _**MC Sher**_ of [_**Gully Boy**_](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt2395469/) (intended as a pun only). _**MC Sher**_ (aka [_Siddhant Chaturvedi_](https://www.imdb.com/name/nm8237564/)) has given a surprising performance in the movie. His introvert role as _Prashant Kanaujia_ in the series [_**Inside Edge**_](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt6112414/) was a job well done then. But his extrovert role of a rapper as _**MC Sher**_ is even better now.

The song [**_Doori_**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QPGmwRNkffc) sung by Ranveer Singh and written by Javed Akhtar caught my attention the most. The music given by _Rishi Rich_ is soothing to the ears. Another song that engaged me was [_**Kab Se Kab Tak**_](https://gaana.com/song/kab-se-kab-tak-1) sung by _Ranveer Singh_ and _Vibha Saraf_. I was never a fan of hip-hop or rap but these two songs redefined rap for me. [_Mikey McCleary_](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mikey_McCleary) and _Ankur Tewari_ did their best to give a retro feel to the hip-hop genre with their song, [_**Jeene Mein Aye Maza**_](https://gaana.com/song/jeene-mein-aye-maza). [_**Mikey McCleary**_](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mikey_McCleary) is known to make remixes of retro hindi classics and also as a music composer to some of the _Lucky Ali’s_ hit songs.

The plot of the movie has nothing new to offer than that of _**8 Mile**_ but still outshines it in Indian context. The setup (cinematography) of the movie is surreal which makes the audience feel at home. The rest is covered by its evenly paced direction and screenplay. The reason why [_**Gully Boy**_](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt2395469/) would still be a popular choice over _**8 Mile**_.
